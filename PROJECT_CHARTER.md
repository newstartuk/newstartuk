## 1. Project Overview

**Project Name:** NewstartUK  
**Tagline:** Your first 90 days, sorted.

**Vision:**  
NewstartUK is a digital companion platform designed to help new immigrants to the UK successfully settle within their first 90 days — providing structured guidance, practical tools, and reliable information tailored to each immigrant's journey.

**MVP 1 Focus:**  
New international students arriving in the UK for the first time.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

New international students arriving in the UK face a overwhelming number of tasks in their first 90 days:

- Opening a UK bank account without a credit history
- Registering with a GP/NHS
- Understanding UK public transport
- Managing a budget in a new currency
- Navigating visa/BRP responsibilities
- Accessing emergency contacts and support services
- Finding housing and understanding tenancy rights

There is no single, reliable, all-in-one platform that guides students through all of this in a structured, jargon-free way.

---

## 3. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goal
Deliver a functional MVP web application that helps new international students complete their essential settling-in tasks within 90 days of arriving in the UK.

### Objectives
- Provide an interactive **90-Day Settlement Checklist** with categorised tasks and progress tracking
- Offer a **Budget Planner** tailored to UK student living costs
- Deliver clear, step-by-step guides for: NHS registration, bank account opening, and emergency contacts
- Display a **Progress Dashboard** showing completion percentage across categories
- Ensure the platform is mobile-responsive and accessible

---

## 4. Success Metrics (MVP 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
