## 🏢 Scrum Framework

NewstartUK follows the **Scrum framework** as defined in the *Scrum Guide (2020)*.  
Scrum is an agile framework for developing, delivering, and sustaining complex products.

---

## 👑 Product Owner (PO)

**Role Holder:** TBD *(User is the Product Owner)*

### Responsibilities

| Area | Duty |
|------|------|
| **Vision** | Owns and articulates the product vision to the team |
| **Backlog** | Creates, maintains, and prioritises the Product Backlog |
| **Strategy** | Defines *what* will be built and why |
| **Value** | Ensures the team works on the highest-value items first |
| **Acceptance** | Accepts or rejects product increments based on Definition of Done |
| **Stakeholder** | Represents the voice of the customer (new immigrants) |
| ** доступ** | Is available to the team for clarifications |

### What the PO Does NOT Do
- Do not assign work or manage the team's tasks
- Do not attend daily stand-ups as a mandatory participant
- Do not micromanage how the work gets done

## 🎯 Scrum Master (SM)

**Role Holder:** TBD

### Responsibilities

| Area | Duty |
|------|------|
| **Ceremonies** | Organises and facilitates all Scrum events (Sprint Planning, Daily Stand-up, Sprint Review, Sprint Retrospective) |
| **Process** | Ensures the team follows Scrum practices correctly |
| **Blockers** | Identifies and removes impediments blocking the team |
| **Coaching** | Coaches the team on agile principles and Scrum |
| **Protection** | Shields the team from external interruptions during the sprint |
| **Improvement** | Promotes continuous improvement in team processes |

### Key Behaviours
- ✅ Acts as a servant-leader, not a manager
- ✅ Facilitates, rather than participates in, team decisions
- ✅ Challenges the status quo constructively
- ❌ Does not assign tasks or dictate solutions

## 👷 Development Team

**Members:** TBD

### Responsibilities

| Area | Duty |
|------|------|
| **Delivery** | Delivers potentially shippable increments each sprint |
| **Estimation** | Participates in backlog refinement and sprint planning |
| **Self-manage** | Organises their own work and decides how to accomplish tasks |
| **Quality** | Adheres to the Definition of Done (DoD) |
| **Transparency** | Communicates progress, blockers, and risks openly |
| **Collaboration** | Works cross-functionally (frontend, backend, design) |

### Team Characteristics
- **Size:** 3–9 people (optimal for MVP 1: 3–4)
- **No hierarchy** — all members are peers
- **Cross-functional** — full-stack capability
- **Self-organising** — no one tells them how to do the work

## 📋 RACI Matrix — Sprint Events

| Event | Product Owner | Scrum Master | Dev Team |
|-------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Sprint Planning | A | R | R |
| Daily Stand-up | I | R | R |
| Sprint Review | R | R | R |
| Sprint Retrospective | I | R | R |
| Backlog Refinement | A | R | C |

> **R** = Responsible | **A** = Accountable | **C** = Consulted | **I** = Informed
